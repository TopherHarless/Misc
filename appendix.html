APPENDIX C: MUSCULAR SYSTEM

The traditional perception of muscles is that they work concentrically and predominantly in one plane of motion. However, to more effectively understand motion, it is imperative to view muscles functioning in all planes of motion and through the entire muscle action spectrum. The following section describes the concentric, eccentric, and isometric actions of the major muscles of the human body.

LOWER LEG MUSCULATURE

ANTERIOR TIBIALIS

  • Concentric Function
  • Concentrically accelerates dorsiflexion and inversion
  • Eccentric Function
  • Eccentrically decelerates plantar flexion and eversion
  • Isometric Function
  • Isometrically stabilizes the arch of the foot

  • Concentric Function
  • Concentrically accelerates plantar flexion and inversion of the foot
  • Eccentric Function
